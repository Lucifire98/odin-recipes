The chocolate Pasta

Chocolate pasta is traditionally just regular pasta enhanced with cocoa powder. While the aroma of chocolate is strong when cooked, the flavor is very subtle. This allows for chocolate pasta to be prepared either savory or sweet

    Ingredient
  • 1 package (10 ounce size) chocolate pasta
  • 3 tablespoons Grand Marnier
  • 1 cup raspberry pie filling
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
    Steps
  1. Cook the pasta as directed on the package. Drain well then rinse in cold water.
  2. Place in the refrigerator while you prepare the toppings
  3. Combine the Grand Marnier and raspberry pie filling until well mixed. Set aside.
  4. Whip the heavy cream with an electric mixer on high speed until soft peaks form. Add the sugar, adding more if desired, and beat until incorporated.
  5. To serve, top the chilled chocolate pasta with the whipped cream and spiked raspberry sauce.
